About

Accountability is the platform where you and your friends can support each other in meeting your goals
and hold each other accountable for bettering yourselves!

How it works:

  • Create a group and share the Group ID with your friends
  • When you log in to your group for the first time, create a personal account, where you can set your goals and choose what you want to improve
  • On your home page, you will see your as well as your group members' progress
  • Send your friends messages to congratulate them if they are doing well or motivate them if they are slacking
  • If you are on track with your goals, you will earn stars!
  • If you need more support, Accountability will send you personalized resources & your friends will receive a notification
