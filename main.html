Accountability

Welcome to Accountability - the platform where you and your friends can support each other in meeting your goals, and hold each other accountable for bettering yourselves!

How it works:
  • One person should create a group and share the Group ID with your friends
  • When you log in to your group for the first time, create a personal account, where you can set your goals and what you want to improve on
  • Once you are all set, you will see your as well as your group members' progress in your home page
  • Send your friends messages to congratulate them if they are doing well or motivate them if they are slacking
  • If you are on track with your goals, you will earn badges!
  • If you need more support, Accountability will send you personalized resources & your friends will receive a notification
